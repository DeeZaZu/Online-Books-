Our Story & Mission

Discover the passion and purpose behind Dee Books Library

Our Mission

Dee Books was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to make great literature accessible to everyone, everywhere. We believe that reading transforms lives, broadens perspectives, and builds stronger communities.

Our team of passionate librarians, technologists, and book lovers work tirelessly to curate the best collection of books and provide the most seamless reading experience possible, regardless of location or circumstance.

We're committed to breaking down barriers to reading by providing free access to thousands of books across all genres, from timeless classics to contemporary bestsellers.

Our Core Values

Accessibility

We believe everyone deserves access to great literature, regardless of location, income, or ability. Our platform is designed to be inclusive and accessible to all.

Education

Reading is fundamental to lifelong learning. We support education by providing resources that inspire curiosity and critical thinking.

Community

We foster a global community of readers who share recommendations, discuss books, and support each other's reading journeys.

Our Journey

2020

Foundation

Dee Books was founded by a group of librarians and tech enthusiasts passionate about making literature accessible to everyone.

2021

First 1,000 Books

We launched our digital library with an initial collection of 1,000 carefully curated books across various genres.

2022

User Community

Our community grew to over 10,000 active readers, with features for book reviews and reading groups.

2023

Mobile App Launch

We expanded our platform with a dedicated mobile app, allowing readers to access books on the go.

2024

10,000 Books Milestone

Our collection reached 10,000 books with new titles added weekly, making us one of the largest free digital libraries.
