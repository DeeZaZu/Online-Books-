About Dee Books

Our Story

LiteraVerse was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make great literature accessible to everyone. We believe that reading transforms lives and should be available to all regardless of location or circumstance.

Our team of passionate librarians, technologists, and book lovers work tirelessly to curate the best collection of books and provide the most seamless reading experience possible.

Our Values

Accessibility: We're committed to making books available to everyone.

Quality: We carefully curate our collection to ensure only the best books make it into our library.

Community: We foster a community of readers who share recommendations and discuss their favorite books.
